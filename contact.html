Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a recipe to my favorites?

To save a recipe to your favorites, simply click the heart icon on any recipe card or recipe detail page. You can access your saved recipes anytime by clicking on the "Favorites" link in the navigation menu.

Can I submit my own recipe?

Yes! We welcome recipe submissions from our community. Please use the contact form on this page and select "Recipe Submission" as the subject. Include your recipe details, and our team will review it.

How can I filter recipes for dietary restrictions?

You can use our category filters to find recipes that match your dietary needs. We have categories for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and more. You can also use the search function to find specific dietary-friendly recipes.

Do you offer meal planning services?

We're currently developing a meal planning feature that will allow you to create weekly meal plans using our recipes. Stay tuned for this exciting update coming soon!
